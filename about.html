About us

Softuni Sports is the best independently owned sports blog in the country. Highly regarded by fans and media members alike, Softuni Sports is an entertaining sports site, written in the form of several blog entries. The site specializes in viral sports content. Content includes controversial and original opinions, news stories, off-beat stories, humorous videos, entertaining photos, exclusive interviews, and witty banter between the writers and commenters.

Softuni Sports was launched yesterday ;) and has been featured and/or cited on several prominent sports sites including MSN, Yahoo!, FOX Sports, ESPN, Sports Illustrated, Sporting News, AOL Sports, CBS Sports, and NBC Sports.

Softuni Sports is a Yardbarker affiliate site, with its content regularly being syndicated by FOX Sports and MSN.
